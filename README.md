## 🧭 What is closeinbox.email?

closeinbox.email is an open-source inbox manager for outbound teams and agencies. It helps you handle replies in one place, sort incoming email by intent, and draft responses with AI help.

It is built for teams that send cold email and need a cleaner way to manage replies. It also supports self-hosted setups, so you can run it on your own system and keep control of your data.

## ✨ What you can do with it

- Keep reply work in one inbox
- Sort inbound email by intent
- Draft replies with AI help
- Track leads and responses
- Set up a self-hosted BYOK workflow
- Use it for outbound sales and agency work
- Support multiple team members in one process

## 📬 Common tasks

### Reply management

Use closeinbox.email to keep reply work in one place. This helps when many leads reply to outbound campaigns and you need a clean way to decide what to do next.

### Intent categorization

The app groups inbound messages by intent. This helps you spot:

- Interested replies
- Questions
- OOO messages
- Not interested replies
- Spam or low-value messages

### AI-assisted drafting

The app can draft reply text for you. You can review the draft, edit it, and send it from your normal email flow.

### Lead management

You can use the app to track leads as they move through your reply process. This gives your team a clearer view of who needs follow-up.

## 🔐 BYOK and self-hosting

## 🛠️ Basic troubleshooting

### The app will not open

- Check that the file finished downloading
- Try opening it again from the Downloads folder
- Make sure your browser is up to date
- Check that Windows is not blocking the file

### You cannot connect your inbox

- Check your email login details
- Make sure the account has permission to connect
- Review any setup steps for Gmail, Outlook, or another provider
- Confirm that your network is working

### AI replies do not appear

- Check your API key
- Make sure the key is pasted in the right place
- Check whether the service you use is enabled
- Try sending one test message first

### The page looks blank

- Refresh the browser
- Clear the cache
- Try another browser
- Check the setup steps again
